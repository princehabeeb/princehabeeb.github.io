TENDON INNOVATIONS

Tendon Innovations is a Nigerian firm involved in Energy Audit and EPC services in the Energy Market. We pride ourselves in designing solutions according to our consumers taste built around quality products and effective backup time for maximum optimization.

We promote sustainability among consumers by providing access to clean energy solutions for residential, Commerce & Industrial and Mini-Grid optimization.

Electric Vehicle Charging Station

We design and install EV Charging Stations in partnership with leading automotive distributing companies in Nigeria.

Solar Systems

We do EPCs services for solar solutions across Nigeria spreading across Residential (Urban and Mini- Grid), Commerce & Industry.
